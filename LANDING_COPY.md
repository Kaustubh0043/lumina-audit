   Yes, Lumina is a free utility powered by Credex to help startups optimize their infra spend.
2. **Do I need to connect my credit card or bank?**
   No. This is a "manual entry" audit. You input your seats and plans, and we do the math. No sensitive data access required.
3. **How is the data stored?**
   Your audit results are stored privately. Shareable URLs strip out company names and emails, showing only the tools and savings numbers.
4. **What is Credex?**
   Credex provides discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your audit shows high savings potential, we'll offer a consultation to help you capture those savings.
5. **How accurate is the pricing?**
   We update our pricing engine weekly based on official vendor data from OpenAI, Anthropic, and others.
