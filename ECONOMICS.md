# Unit Economics — Lumina Audit

## Lead Value to Credex
- **Assumed Credex Revenue Model**: 10-15% commission on credit secondary market sales.
- **Average High-Spend Audit**: $2,000/mo spend.
- **Conversion**: If Credex saves them 30% ($600/mo), Credex earns ~$60–$90/mo in recurring commission.
- **LTV**: Assuming 12-month retention, a converted lead is worth **$720–$1,080**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Organic (X/HN)**: $0 (Time only).
- **Paid (Future)**: If we spent on LinkedIn ads targeting "CTO," a lead (email capture) might cost $15–$25.
- **Blended CAC**: Initial target is <$5 per lead through viral sharing.

## Conversion Funnel Math
- **Audit Completed**: 1,000 users.
- **Email Capture (15% conversion)**: 150 leads.
- **High-Savings Booking (20% of leads)**: 30 consultations.
- **Credit Purchase (30% of bookings)**: 9 customers.
- **Total Revenue (9 * $900 LTV)**: $8,100.
- **Efficiency**: Every 1,000 visitors generates $8k in downstream value.

## Path to $1M ARR
To hit $1M ARR via Lumina:
1. **Volume**: We need ~1,200 paying customers.
2. **Traffic**: Based on our funnel, we need ~130,000 audits completed annually (approx. 10k/month).
3. **Strategic Pivot**: Transition from a "one-time audit" to "Active Spend Monitoring" via an OAuth integration with accounting tools (Quickbooks/Ramp), creating a recurring utility that keeps users coming back to Credex for every new AI subscription.
