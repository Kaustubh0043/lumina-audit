# Unit Economics — Lumina Audit

## Lead Value to Credex
- **Assumed Credex Revenue Model**: 10-15% commission on credit secondary market sales.
- **Average High-Spend Audit**: ₹1,60,000/mo ($2,000) spend.
- **Conversion**: If Credex saves them 30% (₹48,000/mo), Credex earns ~₹4,800–₹7,200/mo in recurring commission.
- **LTV**: Assuming 12-month retention, a converted lead is worth **₹57,600–₹86,400**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Organic (X/HN)**: ₹0 (Time only).
- **Paid (Future)**: If we spent on LinkedIn ads targeting "CTO," a lead (email capture) might cost ₹1,200–₹2,000.
- **Blended CAC**: Initial target is <₹400 per lead through viral sharing.

## Conversion Funnel Math
- **Audit Completed**: 1,000 users.
- **Email Capture (15% conversion)**: 150 leads.
- **High-Savings Booking (20% of leads)**: 30 consultations.
- **Credit Purchase (30% of bookings)**: 9 customers.
- **Total Revenue (9 * ₹70,000 LTV)**: ₹6,30,000.
- **Efficiency**: Every 1,000 visitors generates ₹6.3 Lakhs in downstream value.

## Path to $1M ARR (₹8 Crores)
To hit our growth targets via Lumina:
1. **Volume**: We need ~1,200 paying customers.
2. **Traffic**: Based on our funnel, we need ~1,30,000 audits completed annually (approx. 10k/month).
3. **Strategic Pivot**: Transition from a "one-time audit" to "Active Spend Monitoring" via an OAuth integration with accounting tools (Tally/RazorpayX), creating a recurring utility that keeps users coming back to Credex for every new AI subscription.
